Steps of Recycling Plastic: Step-By-Step

    Step 1

  • Gather all your plastic waste. This includes bottles, containers, bags, and any other plastic items you use.

    Step 2

  • Separate plastics from other types of waste. Rinse out any food or liquid residues from plastic containers to prevent contamination.

    Step 3

  • Sort plastics by type. Different types of plastics have different recycling processes. Check for the recycling symbols and numbers on the plastics (e.g., PET #1, HDPE #2).

    Step 4

  • Find your local recycling program or drop-off center. Many cities have curbside recycling programs where you can leave your sorted plastics.

    Step 5

  • At the recycling facility, the plastics are further sorted, cleaned, and processed into raw materials that can be used to manufacture new products.

    Step 6

  • Support the recycling loop by purchasing products made from recycled plastic. Look for items with recycled content.
