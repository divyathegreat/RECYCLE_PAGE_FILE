Steps of Recycling Metal Can: Step-By-Step

    Step 1

  • Empty the contents of the can and give it a good rinse to remove any food residue. This prevents contamination in the recycling process.

    Step 2

  • Peel off any paper labels if possible. Most recycling facilities can handle labels, but it's a good practice to remove them if you can.

    Step 3

  • If space is a concern, you can flatten the can to save room in your recycling bin. It can help maximize space.

    Step 4

  • Check your local recycling guidelines to see if metal cans need to be sorted separately from other recyclables like plastic and paper.

    Step 5

  • Place the rinsed, flattened can in your recycling bin if your local program supports curbside collection. If curbside collection is not available, find the nearest recycling center and take your metal cans there.
