Steps of Recycling Paper: Step-By-Step

    Step 1

  • Gather all your paper waste, such as newspapers, magazines, office paper, cardboard, and paper packaging

    Step 2

  • Sort the paper by type. While many recycling programs accept mixed paper, some require separation of different types

    Step 3

  • Remove any non-paper materials such as staples, plastic wrappers, and food residues. Clean paper is more easily recycled.

    Step 4

  • Flatten cardboard boxes and large paper items to save space in your recycling bin.

    Step 5

  • Verify the recycling guidelines in your area. Some places have specific rules about what types of paper can be recycled and how it should be prepared.

    Step 6

  • If your area offers curbside recycling, place the bin at the curb on the scheduled collection day. OR If curbside collection is not available, take your paper to a nearby recycling center.

    Step 7

  • Make recycling a regular habit. Continuously collect, sort, and recycle your paper waste.
